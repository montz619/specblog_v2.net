Key Specifications

Disclaimer: All specifications are compiled from publicly available sources and manufacturer announcements. SpecBlog.net is not affiliated with Samsung. Product names and trademarks are property of their respective owners.

Price (Base Model): $879 (as of July 2025)
Display: 6.9 inches, (~92.5% screen-to-body ratio), Dynamic LTPO AMOLED 2X, 120Hz, HDR10+, 2600 nits (peak), 1440 x 3120 pixels, 19.5:9 ratio (~498 ppi density), Corning Gorilla Armor 2, DX anti-reflective coating
Chipset: Qualcomm SM8750-AB Snapdragon 8 Elite (3 nm)
CPU: Octa-core (2x4.47 GHz Oryon V2 Phoenix L + 6x3.53 GHz Oryon V2 Phoenix M)
GPU: Adreno 830
RAM: 12GB / 16GB
Storage: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, 1TB, No SD Card slots
Rear Camera:
  • Wide: 200 MP, f/1.7, 24mm, 1/1.3", 0.6µm, multi-directional PDAF, OIS
  • Ultra-Wide: 50 MP, f/1.9, 120˚, 1/2.5", 0.7µm, dual pixel PDAF, Super Steady video
  • Periscope Telephoto: 50 MP, f/3.4, 111mm, 1/2.52", 0.7µm, PDAF, OIS, 5x optical zoom
  • Telephoto: 10 MP, f/2.4, 67mm, 1/3.52", 1.12µm, PDAF, OIS, 3x optical zoom
  • Features: Laser AF, Best Face, LED flash, auto-HDR, panorama
  • Video: 8K@24/30fps, 4K@30/60/120fps, 1080p@30/60/120/240fps, HDR10+, gyro-EIS
Front Camera:
  • Single: 12 MP, f/2.2, 26mm (wide), dual pixel PDAF
  • Features: HDR, HDR10+
  • Video: 4K@30/60fps, 1080p@30fps
Network: GSM / CDMA / HSPA / EVDO / LTE / 5G
Sim Card: Nano-SIM + Nano-SIM + eSIM + eSIM - INT, Nano-SIM + eSIM + eSIM (max 2 at a time) - USA
Battery: Non-removable Li-Ion 5000 mAh, 45W Super Fast Charging, 15W wireless (Qi2 Ready), 4.5W reverse wireless
Operating System: Android 15, One UI 7
Connectivity: 5G, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, NFC, UWB
Dimensions: 162.8 x 77.6 x 8.2 mm
Weight: 218 g
Features:
  • Sensors: Fingerprint (under display, ultrasonic), accelerometer, gyro, proximity, compass, barometer
  • Extras: Samsung DeX, Samsung Wireless DeX (Desktop Mode)
Colors: Titanium Silver Blue, Titanium Black, Titanium White Silver, Titanium Gray, Titanium Jade Green, Titanium Jet Black, Titanium Pink Gold
