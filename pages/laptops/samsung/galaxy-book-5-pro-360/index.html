Samsung Galaxy Book 5 Pro 360

A versatile 2-in-1 laptop with premium design, stunning AMOLED display, and powerful performance.

Key Specifications

Price (Base model): About $1,699
Display: 16" Dynamic AMOLED 2X (2880 x 1800), 120Hz, 400 nits, multitouch, S Pen support
Processor: Intel Core Ultra 7 155H (up to 4.8 GHz, 16 cores)
RAM: 16 GB LPDDR5X (onboard)
Storage: 512 GB NVMe SSD (expandable)
Graphics: Intel Arc Graphics
Ports: 2x USB-C 4.0 (Thunderbolt 4), 1x USB-A 3.2, 1x microSD card slot, 1x 3.5mm headphone jack
Battery: 76 Wh Li-ion
Operating System: Windows 11 Home
Wireless: Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax), Bluetooth 5.3
Camera: FHD (1080p) webcam with IR for Windows Hello
Dimensions: 355.4 x 229.8 x 13.8 mm
Weight: 1.69 kg
Color: Moonstone Gray

For more information, visit the official Samsung website.
