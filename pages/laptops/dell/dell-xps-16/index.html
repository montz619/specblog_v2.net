Dell XPS 16 9640

A perfect blend of performance and elegant design, ideal for creative professionals and power users.

Key Specifications

Price (Base model): About $3,732
Display: 16.3″ FHD+ (1920 x 1200) Infinity Edge, Non-Touch, Anti-Glare, 500-nits
Processor: Intel® Core™ Ultra9 processor 185H (24MB Cache, 16 cores, up to 5.1 GHz)
RAM: 32GB, LPDDR5X, 6400MT/s Onboard
Storage: 512GB M.2 PCIe NVMe Solid State Drive
Graphics: NVIDIA® GeForce RTX™ 4060, 8 GB GDDR6
Ports: 2x Thunderbolt 4.0 port with Power Delivery and DisplayPort, microSD-card slot v6.0, 1x Thunderbolt 4.0 port with Power Delivery and DisplayPort, Headphone and speaker port
Battery: 6-Cell, 99.5Whr with 130W Type-C Adapter
Operating System: Windows 11 Home, Single Language English
Wireless: Intel® Killer™ Wi-Fi 7 1750 (BE200) 2×2 + Bluetooth 5.4 Wireless Card
Camera: FHD (1080p) webcam, Windows Hello compliant, Express Sign-In, Intel IPU6
Dimensions: Height: 18.7 mm (0.74”), Width: 358.1 mm (14.1”), Depth: 240.0mm (9.4”)
Weight: 2.13kg (4.7lbs.) for FHD+ and UMA; 2.20kg (4.8lbs) for OLED
Audio: Quad-speaker design with 3W Main x 2 Channel + 2W Tweeter x 2 Channel; 10W total peak output, Studio quality tuning with Waves MaxxAudio® Pro and Waves Nx® 3D audio, Dolby Atmos
Colors: Platinum
