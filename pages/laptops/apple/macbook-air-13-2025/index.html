MacBook Air (13-inch, M4, 2025)

Apple's powerhouse laptop, designed for professionals and creatives demanding ultimate performance and a stunning display.

Key Specifications

Price (Base model): $1,160
Display: Liquid Retina display, 13.6-inch (diagonal) LED-backlit display with IPS technology;2 2560-by-1664 native resolution at 224 pixels per inch, 500 nits brightness
Processor: Apple M4 chip, 10‑core CPU with 4 performance cores and 6 efficiency cores
GPU: 8‑core GPU, 10‑core GPU, Hardware-accelerated ray tracing
RAM: 16GB unified memory, Configurable to: 24GB or 32GB
Storage: 256GB SSD, Configurable to: 512GB, 1TB, or 2TB
Ports: MagSafe 3 charging port, 3.5 mm headphone jack, Two Thunderbolt 4 (USB-C) ports with support for: Charging, DisplayPort, Thunderbolt 4 (up to 40Gb/s), USB 4 (up to 40Gb/s)
Battery: Built-in 53.8‑watt‑hour lithium‑polymer battery, Fast-charge capable with 70W USB-C Power Adapter
Operating System: macOS - with Apple Intelligence
Wireless: Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax)5, Bluetooth 5.3
Camera: 12MP Center Stage camera with support for Desk View, 1080p HD video recording, Advanced image signal processor with computational video
Audio: Four-speaker sound system, Spatial Audio, Dolby Atmos
Dimensions: Height: 0.44 inch (1.13 cm), Width: 11.97 inches (30.41 cm), Depth: 8.46 inches (21.5 cm)
Weight: Weight: 2.7 pounds (1.24 kg)7
Keyboard: Backlit Magic Keyboard 78 (U.S.) or 79 (ISO) keys, Touch ID, Force Touch trackpad
Colors: Sky Blue, Silver, Starlight, Midnight

For more information, visit the official Apple website.
