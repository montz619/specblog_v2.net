ZTE Nubia RedMagic Nova

Also known as ZTE nubia Red Magic Nova Gaming Tablet, Zte nubia Red Magic Gaming Tablet Pro.

Key Specifications

Disclaimer: All specifications are compiled from publicly available sources and manufacturer announcements. SpecBlog.net is not affiliated with ZTE Corporation. Product names and trademarks are property of their respective owners.

Price (Base Model): 500 EUR
Display: IPS LCD, 144Hz, 550 nits, 10.9 inches, (~82.6% screen-to-body ratio), 1800 x 2880 pixels, 16:10 ratio (~312 ppi density)
Chipset: Qualcomm SM8650-AC Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (4 nm)
CPU: 8-core (1x3.4GHz Cortex-X4 & 3x3.1GHz Cortex-A720 & 2x2.9GHz Cortex-A720 & 2x2.2GHz Cortex-A520)
GPU: Adreno 750 (1 GHz)
RAM: 12GB, 16GB, 24GB
Storage: 256GB, 512GB, 1TB
Rear Camera:
  • Single: 50 MP, f/1.8, 26mm (wide)
  • Features: LED flash, HDR, panorama
  • Video: 4K@30fps, 1080p@30fps
Front Camera:
  • Single: 20 MP, f/2.0, 26mm (wide)
  • Video: 1080p@30fps
Network: No cellular connectivity
Sim Card: No sim card slot
Battery: 10100 mAh, 80W wired, 100% in 55 min - International model, 120W wired, 50% in 15 min, 100% in 45 min - China model
Operating System: Android 14, Redmagic OS 9.5
Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/6, dual-band, Bluetooth 5.4, A2DP, LE, NFC
Dimensions: 253.3 x 164.6 x 7.3 mm (9.97 x 6.48 x 0.29 in)
Weight: 530 g (1.17 lb)
Features:
  • Sensors: Fingerprint (side-mounted), accelerometer, gyro, proximity, compass
  • Extras: RGB light on the back, Built-in cooling fan (Illuminated), Aviation-grade aluminum frame
Colors: Cyclone, Snowfall, Midnight
